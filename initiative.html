About The Initiative

Cyber•Data Guild is a responsibility-driven peer learning community designed to help learners build real-world skills in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and data analytics through self-paced learning, weekly accountability, and hands-on projects.

The initiative emphasizes portfolio building, practical assignments, and peer review, rather than certificate collection. We believe in creating a supportive environment where members challenge each other to grow and deliver quality work.
